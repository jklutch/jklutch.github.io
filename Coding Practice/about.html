About

My work examines the concepts of process and play through illustrative and technical media. I explore the whimsical nature of early adulthood through anthropomorphized characters. These characters are placed in settings real and unreal within my everyday life and landscape. I seek resemblance between these characters and people who play an active role in the Minneapolis neighborhoods I traverse. Ocassionally I choose to represent myself and others through photo-realistic portraiture.

I gravitate towards printmaking processes and drawing. During my time at the University of Minnesota I studied these disciplines along with ceramics and painting, and graduated with a Bachelors in Art.
